How SolPlace Works

Getting Started

SolPlace is a collaborative pixel art canvas on Solana where creativity meets blockchain technology.

  • Connect your Phantom wallet
  • Ensure you have $SPLACE tokens
  • Click any pixel on the canvas
  • Select your desired color
  • Pay 10,000 $SPLACE to place your pixel

Deflationary Mechanism

Every pixel placement burns 10,000 $SPLACE tokens, making the token deflationary. The tokens are permanently removed from circulation, increasing scarcity over time.
